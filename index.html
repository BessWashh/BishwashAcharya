People I Look Up To

Idols

YB
Yama Buddha
Rapper · Poet · Legend

Yama Buddha is one of my greatest inspirations. As the architect of NepHop and a voice of truth, he showed me that music can be more than entertainment — it can educate, empower, and unite people. His words, vision, and authenticity continue to influence the way I think about creativity and self-expression. Though he is no longer with us, his message still resonates deeply within me and countless others. His legacy lives on, and he will always be remembered with respect, gratitude, and admiration.

Neymar Junior
Footballer · Artist · Free Spirit

To me, Neymar Jr. represents the beauty of football at its purest form — creativity, joy, and passion. His artistry on the pitch, unwavering confidence, and dedication to his craft continue to inspire me every day. He reminds me that greatness is achieved not only through talent but through perseverance, courage, and an unshakable belief in oneself. He is more than a footballer to me — he is a symbol of passion, resilience, and excellence.
