Who I Am

Design is the
language I speak

Designer

Hello! I'm Bishwash Acharya, a passionate Graphic Designer from the heart of Jhapa, Nepal. My journey with design began with a fascination for how visuals can carry emotion, tell stories, and move people — often without a single word.

Over the years, I've honed a craft that sits at the intersection of strategy and aesthetics. I don't just make things look good — I make them work. Every color choice, every type pairing, every composition is a deliberate decision in service of the message.

Whether you're a startup looking to make your mark, an established brand ready to evolve, or a creative looking for a collaborator who thinks as deeply as they design — I'm that person. My work has helped clients elevate their presence, command attention, and build lasting recognition in competitive markets.

Based in Arjundhara, Jhapa, I bring a global design sensibility with local roots — and I believe that's what makes my work genuinely distinctive.
