The Manifesto

I am a graphic designer, lyricist, rapper, and poet—navigating the intersections of raw sound and visual storytelling. My relationship with technology defines my philosophy: I am the guy who loves computers not simply to work with them, but to relentlessly experiment with how they can be pushed.


As a proud higher education dropout, I traded institutional templates for real-world chaos. I spend every single day executing unconventional ideas and embracing creative friction. I am intimately familiar with battling my own internal struggles, but giving up has never been an option. Every piece of art, every verse, and every line of code is proof of resilience.
